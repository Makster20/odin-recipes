Fried Chicken

Description

This is fried chicken, a very nice and delicious food to eat with almost anything!!!!!!!!!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Seasoning
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Clean the chicken.
  2. Preheat the oil for cooking by setting it in a pot.
  3. Mix the breadcrumbs and seasoning together, and coat the chicken with it.
  4. Drop the chicken in the oil. If you touch the oil it will tickle you so be careful (it will make you laugh).
  5. Once Golden, remove from oil and enjoy burning hot.
